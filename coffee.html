Amazing Coffee combo

These are fun amazing coffee combos you can make at home! coffee everyday coffee everywhere! YOU have every excuse to have a beautiful cup of coffee! made with Love.




COFFEE + CHOCOLATE

When coffee and chocolate meet we have a mocha. A mocha is somewhere between a hot chocolate and a latte, combining cocoa and expresso coffee. Mochas are perfect if you're in the mood for coffee but would like something a little sweeter and more indulgent. blend your favorite type of chocolate and coffee to get this combo.

COFFEE + ICE CREAM

When coffee and ice cream meet it becomes a spark for the ice coffee lovers. Its somewhere between an iced coffee and a milk shake with extra flavour from your choice of ice cream flavour, giving it a smooth and relaxing taste. blend your ice cream with your ice coffee and regulate the consistency with milk to get your perfect combo.

COFFEE + COCONUT

When coffee meets coconut the two flavours blend together perfectly. There is something natural, but subly, sweet about both coffee and coconut, if you like your coffee milky, why not add a dribble of coconut milk or creamed coconut. Stir it in or blend it in for a smooth, creamy finish. The coconut seems to enhance and almost intensify the coffee flavours instesd of masking any of them.

COFFEE + CINNAMON

When coffee meets cinnamon the drink is a little sweeter and earthier, which accents coffee's natural flavours perfectly. But only add a tiny sprinkle of cinnamon powder to your coffee or you'll ruin it, as this seeminly innocuous powder packs a massive wallop. you can also use cinnamon syrup instead to give a more satisfying result.
