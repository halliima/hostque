The Experience

Coffee shop was established in the year 1990 by Mrs Maria bello, at the age of 23, maria was able to achieve her dream of opening a 24hours coffee shop and was able to successfuly run the coffee shop all on her own with the support of her parents(Mr&Mrs johnson).

Coffee shop has been in business for 30years! Now being run by Mrs Anaya the first daughter to Maria, who later upgraded the coffee shop by adding a breakfast, lunch and dessert Menu. Anaya being a chef and a culinary expact is handing the business so well with all the amazing recipes of her making, but Anaya still has not changed the ancient touch Maria made in the coffee shop, Hence the name COFFEE SHOP has not being altered.
